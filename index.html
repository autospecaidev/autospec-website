How it works

From unstructured data to structured, actionable specs

Our AI agent ingests data from any source, in any format, and transforms it into a single intelligent taxonomy — ready to share with anyone, in any format they require.

01
📥

Ingest from any source

Connect your suppliers, co-manufacturers, ERP, PLM, or QMS. AutoSpec.ai accepts unstructured data in any format — spreadsheets, PDFs, emails, or API — eliminating manual normalization.

02
⚡

AI normalizes & validates

Our AI agent interprets, structures, and validates your product data — calculating nutritional values, verifying allergen declarations, checking label claims, and mapping to regulatory requirements automatically.

03
📤

Deliver in any format

Push validated specifications to any customer or partner in whatever format they require — PDF, Excel, API, or directly to their supplier portal. One source of truth, infinite outputs.
