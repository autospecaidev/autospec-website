1. Who we are

Autospec.ai is an AI-powered food specification management platform. References to "we", "us" or "our" refer to Autospec.ai. Our contact email is info@autospec.ai.

2. What data we collect

When you visit autospec.ai we may collect the following information, only with your consent:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Your approximate country and city (not precise location)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • How you arrived at our site (e.g. search engine, direct link)

When you submit a demo or quote request we collect your name, work email address, company name, and any message you choose to include.

3. How we use your data

Analytics data is used solely to understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it. Contact form data is used only to respond to your enquiry. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

4. Legal basis (GDPR)

For analytics cookies we rely on your consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR). You may withdraw consent at any time by clicking "Decline" in the cookie banner or clearing your browser cookies. For contact form submissions we rely on legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) to respond to your enquiry.

5. Third-party services

Google Analytics 4, used to analyse website traffic. Data is processed by Google LLC. Google may transfer data outside the EEA under Standard Contractual Clauses. Analytics only activates with your consent. Google Privacy Policy →

Formspree, used to receive contact form submissions. Your form data is transmitted securely to Formspree and forwarded to our team email. Formspree Privacy Policy →

6. Data retention

Analytics data is retained for 14 months (Google Analytics default). Contact form enquiries are retained in our email for as long as necessary to respond and for legitimate business record-keeping.

7. Your rights under GDPR

If you are in the EEA or UK, you have the right to: access your personal data, request correction or deletion, object to processing, withdraw consent at any time, and lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. To exercise any right, email info@autospec.ai.

8. Cookies we use

Strictly necessary: A single cookie (cookieChoice) stored in your browser to remember your consent preference. No expiry; persists until you clear your browser data.

Analytics (with consent only): Google Analytics sets cookies (_ga, _ga_*) to distinguish unique visitors and sessions. These expire after 2 years and 24 hours respectively.

For any privacy-related questions, email us at info@autospec.ai. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
